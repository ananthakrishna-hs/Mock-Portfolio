Cricket is my oxygen. It is my hobby and my interest. Only bad thing that has happened is not turning it to profession. But thats life!

Generally, I don't take things too emotional. The only time I do, even in the remotest way possible is by watching a cricket match or a superb movie. Let's talk more.

Cricket
The sport, not an insect.

I learnt a lot from cricket, believe me!

I'm not like any other fan, you know criticise when your team loses or go mad when they win. But I am a human I feel those but I know the difficulties behind it, again believe me. By learning I mean values of life. Yes you heard it right, the sport can teach you values of life more than you can think of. I am not saying I have given up my dream of being a cricketer but making sure that I live not survive if something doesn't go in my way.

Anyway I do hope for a miracle rather a slightest chance, to try atleast.

What have I done with respect to cricket?

  • Tried to get involved(still).
  • Made a social network page for entertainment of fans(About 200).
  • Get inspired.
